Penguins!

Penguins are in a variety of different places. They live in Antarctica(obviously), South Africa, and the Galapagos.

Penguin feet don't hae any protection. So how do they protect themselves? The answer is in their blood circulation. The warm blood goes down to the feet, gets cold, but when that happens, the cold blood vessel wraps around the warm blood vessel and gets warm again.
